Why Do I Need Samsung Drivers On My PC Anyway?
Your phone will not be properly identified by Windows if you won't install it,
after the installation you would be able to access its internal-storage and sd-card
or use your phone as a USB-modem.
- You can use your phone as a camera for your PC.
- Using Odin Wikipedia | Download in-order to flash a firmware, requires switching your phone to download-mode and to be connected to the PC, you'll need the drivers so Odin will identify your phone's-modem.
- Debugging, using Andorid Debug Bridge (ADB) and Android software-development.

How to reverse-engineer it, yourself! [Tips]


  1. The Kies3Setup.exe is visual-c++ v8, InstallShield setup, you can use InstallShield /b switch to extract its content.

  2. This is a MSI, a very friendly Windows-Installer package. You can even slipstream it into your custom Windows installation :)
    Use msi2xml to extract its content.

  3. Under the folder-tree you'll find SAMSUNG_USB_Driver_for_Mobile_Phones.exe, it's a Nullsoft installer (NSIS), use either NSIDis or simply open it with 7Zip.
    Easy.
